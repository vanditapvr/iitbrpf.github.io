Celebrating 5 years of ASPIRE

On 28th June 2021, IIT Bombay Research Park Foundation celebrated the completion of 5 successful years since its inception in 2016. What had then started with a vision to bring industry and academia together has been thriving today on maturing science and technology.

28th June, 2021

Tech Meet_1.0

On 8th May, 2021, a virtual meeting between the Institute Technical Council and Research Park Clients was organized by IITB Research Park Foundation. Prof. Ravindra Gudi, Principal- in-Charge, IITB Research Park gave the opening remarks and welcomed representatives from Student cells and key functionaries from the client side.

8th May, 2021

Research park- Clients Day

The first edition of Research Park – Clients Day was held on 11th December 2019. It was a half-day event, the purpose of which was to introduce the clients of Research Park to each other. IITB Research Park clients shared their experience so far with the Research Park and IIT Bombay.

11th December, 2019

RESEARCHx 2019

The first edition of RESEARCHx was held on October 20th, 2019. The all-day event was organized by IITB Research Park and the Undergraduate Academic Council.

20th October, 2019

IITBRP Symposium on "Data for Good"

IITB Research Park in association with Facebook is organizing a Symposium on "Data for Good" (a Facebook initiative). This initiative aims to bring together leading academicians, researchers, and development organizations to discuss the evolving role of data in solving social challenges.

23rd July, 2019

Diversity in the Gaming Industry

Ubisoft in collaboration with IITB Research Park is organizing a conference on “Diversity in Gaming” on 10th January 2019 to discuss the diverse profiles and jobs prevalent in the Gaming Industry.
