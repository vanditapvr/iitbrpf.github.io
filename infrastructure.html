“Ideas come effortlessly in a calm and serene environment, and this is what ASPIRE is all about”.

ASPIRE is cocooned in the flora and fauna rich campus of IIT Bombay. Nestled among the hills and the tranquil waters of the Powai and Vihar lake on one side, and the hustle of the commercial Powai Plaza on the other, ASPIRE is a perfect place for nature and urban to commingle. The proximity to IIT Bombay, which delivers some of the brilliant innovation stories to the country, is what drives industries to set up their R&D centres here.

The Research Park is spread across a sprawling area of 49,500 square metre. With 14 floors, heavy load-bearing capacity, and a green building, we provide modern facilities to support industries keen on innovation and developing solutions.
