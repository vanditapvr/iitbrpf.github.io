SIG - Pharma, Biotech, Chemical | energy | semiconductor and hardware





Mumbai May 3, 2016:- The IITB Research Park Foundation is organizing a set of sector Specific Interest Group (SIG) workshops on 3rd May, 2016, in the following sectors where IIT Bombay has demonstrated strength: 1.Energy 2.Semiconductors and Electronics Hardware 3.Chemicals, Pharmaceuticals and Biotechnology The objective of the workshops will be to identify R&D themes that are of mutual interest and to seek sectoral inputs for defining the Research Park
